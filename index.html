Tournament Results

The relative strength of each competitor overall and on each attribute is estimated with the Bradley-Terry model. It uses the wins and losses of each competitor to estimate their strength. The strength of each competitor is scaled such that the strongest competitor has a strength of 100. The benefit over a simple win-loss ratio is that it accounts for the strength of the opponents.
